Mysore, Karnataka

Experience the cultural capital of Karnataka, known for its royal heritage, intricate architecture, and vibrant traditions. Mysore offers a perfect blend of history and charm.

Popular Attractions:

  • Mysore Palace
  • Chamundi Hill and Chamundeshwari Temple
  • Brindavan Gardens
  • St. Philomena’s Church
  • Railway Museum
  • Karanji Lake
  • Mysore Zoo
  • Bull temple
  • Jaganmohan Palace Art Gallery

Activities to Enjoy:

  • Heritage walks, exploring local markets, enjoying Mysore Dasara festivities, and savoring Mysore Pak.

Coorg, Karnataka

Known for its coffee plantations, misty hills, and serene landscapes, Coorg is a perfect getaway for nature and adventure lovers.

Popular Attractions:

  • Abbey Falls
  • Dubare Elephant Camp
  • Raja’s Seat
  • Talacauvery

Activities to Enjoy:

  • Coffee plantation tours, elephant interactions, trekking, river rafting

Ooty, Tamil Nadu

Ooty, the "Queen of Hill Stations," is famous for its tea gardens, botanical gardens, and Nilgiri Mountain Railway.

Popular Attractions:

  • Botanical Gardens
  • Ooty Lake
  • Nilgiri Mountain Railway
  • Dodabetta Peak

Activities to Enjoy:

  • Boating, trekking, visiting tea plantations

Pondicherry

Pondicherry offers French colonial charm, serene beaches, and a unique blend of Indian and European cultures.

Popular Attractions:

  • Promenade Beach
  • Auroville
  • Sri Aurobindo Ashram
  • French Quarter

Activities to Enjoy:

  • Beach walks, yoga, cultural exploration

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its ancient ruins, temples, and stunning landscapes along the Tungabhadra River.

Popular Attractions:

  • Virupaksha Temple
  • Stone Chariot
  • Vittala Temple
  • Matanga Hill

Activities to Enjoy:

  • Exploring ancient ruins, photography, trekking

  • Soochipara Waterfalls
  • Pookode Lake

Popular Attractions:

Activities to Enjoy:

Popular Attractions:

Activities to Enjoy:

  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  • Sarnath
  • Assi Ghat

Activities to Enjoy:

  • Boat rides, evening aarti, exploring ancient temples, spiritual tours
