Top Tourist Destinations in India

Mysore, Karnataka

The Crown Jewel of Karnataka's Royal Heritage.

Coorg, Karnataka

Coorg is known for its coffee plantations, misty hills, waterfalls like Abbey Falls, and serene landscapes perfect for nature lovers.

Ooty, Tamil Nadu

The "Queen of Hill Stations," Ooty is famous for its tea gardens, botanical gardens, and the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.

Pondicherry

Pondicherry offers French colonial charm, serene beaches, and a unique blend of Indian and European cultures.

Munnar, Kerala

Munnar is a serene hill station known for its tea gardens, lush green hills, and beautiful viewpoints such as Top Station and Echo Point.

Hampi, Karnataka

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its ancient ruins, temples, and stunning landscapes along the Tungabhadra River.
