General Services

What types of travel services does Savitr Travels offer?

We provide personalized travel itineraries, guided tours, transportation arrangements, hotel bookings, and group travel services, specializing in South Indian destinations.

Can I customize my travel package?

Absolutely! We offer tailored travel plans to suit your preferences, whether you’re looking for a family trip, a romantic getaway, or an adventure tour.

Tour Details

What destinations do you cover?

Our expertise lies in Karnataka’s iconic destinations, including the majestic Mysore Palace, the ancient ruins of Hampi, the stunning hill station of Coorg, the beautiful beaches of Gokarna, and the spiritual town of Udupi. Beyond Karnataka, we extend our services across India, from the serene backwaters of Kerala to the majestic Himalayas, ensuring a comprehensive and unforgettable travel experience.

Are your tours guided?

Yes, we provide expert guides who are knowledgeable about the history, culture, and hidden gems of each destination.
